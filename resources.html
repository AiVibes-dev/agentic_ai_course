Learning Resources

This page provides a comprehensive collection of resources and references to support your learning throughout the Agentic AI Systems Architect course. These materials are organized by module and include academic papers, books, online courses, documentation, tools, and community resources.

General Resources

Books

  • Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig - Foundational text covering core AI concepts
  • Designing Machine Learning Systems by Chip Huyen - Practical guide to building production ML systems
  • Building Intelligent Agents by Deborah L. McGuinness - Overview of agent architectures and development
  • Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto - Comprehensive coverage of RL techniques
  • Designing Autonomous AI Systems by Andrew Ng and Fei-Fei Li - Modern approaches to autonomous systems

Online Courses

  • Deep Learning Specialization - Andrew Ng's comprehensive deep learning course
  • Full Stack Deep Learning - End-to-end ML system development
  • Practical AI for Developers - Applied AI development techniques
  • Reinforcement Learning Specialization - Comprehensive RL curriculum

Communities and Forums

  • Hugging Face Community - Active community for ML and LLM development
  • AI Alignment Forum - Discussions on AI safety and alignment
  • r/MachineLearning - Reddit community for ML discussions
  • LangChain Discord - Community for LangChain developers
  • AI Stack Exchange - Q&A platform for AI topics

Blogs and Newsletters
